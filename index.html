about us

BROP — Be Real, Own Prints — is a bold and creative clothing design brand based in (pink city) Jaipur. Founded by Suru and Ganesh, BROP was born out of a passion to let people express their unique identity through customized and trend-forward T-shirts. Whether you're creating your own design or choosing from our exclusive collection, every piece we craft is made to reflect individuality, confidence, and creativity. At BROP, we don’t just print clothes — we bring your imagination to life, one design at a time.
